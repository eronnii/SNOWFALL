Franklin Saint

Franklin Saint is a central character in the TV series "Snowfall," which follows his life and the rise of the crack epidemic in Los Angeles during the 1980s. Franklin begins as a young, ambitious drug dealer looking to make a name for himself in the world of narcotics. However, as his business grows, he becomes increasingly entangled in the dangerous and violent world of drug trafficking. Throughout the series, Franklin faces numerous challenges, both personal and professional, as he navigates the complexities of the drug trade. He forms alliances with other criminals, including his uncle Jerome and friend Leon, but also faces threats from rival gangs and law enforcement. Franklin's character is complex, as he grapples with the moral implications of his actions and the impact they have on his community. Despite his initial intentions to provide for his family and build a better life, Franklin must confront the destructive nature of the drug trade and the toll it takes on those around him. As the series progresses, Franklin's journey becomes increasingly fraught with danger, betrayal, and loss, highlighting the harsh realities of life in the midst of a drug epidemic. His story is one of ambition, struggle, and ultimately, the consequences of choices made in pursuit of power and success.
